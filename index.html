What is "The Bird Says" and "Spider Cola" ?

Spider Cola and The Bird Says are character accessories in the Roblox game that will be visible on the shoulder of your avatar!

To get these accessories, you need to enter a secret promo code! Read the instructions below to get promo codes :)



Instruction

  • Go to the tab "Promo Codes"
  • Answer a few questions
  • Pass the captcha
  • Get your promo codes!

Note

All promo codes are old, they work and were created by roblox itself!

"The Bird Says____." was added to the game in 2015 and "Spider Cola" was added on May 2019. Promo codes still work
